# Privacy Policy for YummyLabs

**Effective Date: April 2026**

## Data Collection
YummyLabs does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app data (cake configurations, favourites, business details, and settings) is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server.

## Third-Party Services
YummyLabs uses Apple's StoreKit framework to process in-app purchases. Payment information is handled entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's privacy policy. YummyLabs does not have access to your payment details.

## Analytics & Tracking
YummyLabs does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. No third-party SDKs are included in the app.

## Data Sharing
YummyLabs does not share any data with third parties. PDF exports and quotes are generated locally on your device and are only shared when you choose to do so using the iOS share sheet.

## Children's Privacy
YummyLabs does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app contains no user accounts, social features, or content that targets children.

## Changes to This Policy
If this privacy policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

## Contact
For questions about this privacy policy, contact: yummylabsapp@gmail.com
